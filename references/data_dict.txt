Ask A Manager Salary Survey 2019 (Responses)
Source: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1rGCKXIKt-7l5gX06NAwO3pjqEHh-oPXtB8ihkp0vGWo/edit#gid=382484678

Variables:

- timestamp: Time the entry was recorded
- age: Age of the person entering the record
- industry: Industry that the person works in
- job_title: job title of the person putting in the entry
- annual_salary: annual salary of the person putting in the entry
- currency: currency (i.e., USD, CNY, etc.)
- location: Location of their job
- work_years: Number of years of work post-college.
- notes: If your job title needs additional context, please clarify here
- other_currency: If "Other currency", please indicate the currency here
